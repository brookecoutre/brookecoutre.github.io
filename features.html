Why Support Us?

We pride ourselves on providing pure, raw honey locally.

There are many steps to get the precious honey from the hives, bottled, and in your hand.


First, the boxes need to be full of honey! The bees get the first box for themselves so the queen can lay eggs and make more worker bees. When the top boxes are full of comb, it is time to harvest

Second, someone has to go and extract the honey comb from the hives. The best time is morning or early evening when the bees are more calm before or after a work day. Extraction involves removing the boxes.

Third, the honeycomb needs to be seperated from the frames. We do this by uncaping the wax layer on honeycomb. We then spin in through a machine which causes the honey come fall out.

Fourth, the honey is all set in a large tub to be poured! The frames go back in their boxes with the leftover wax and the bees can start making more honey from there.

Fifth, the honey is ready to be put in jars. We put the honey in a variety of jars, but for longer term storage, we have found large mason jars to be our favorite.

Lastly, we place our signiture label on each bottle or jar. Now time to enjoy!

How can honey benefit me?

Natural Sweetner

Honey is a natural sweetner which means less sugar can be added to achieve the same sweetness as sugar. Honey goes great in your cup of tea or even in your recipes.

Fights Allergies

Buying and consuming local honey in the area you reside helps fight allergies by letting your immune system adjust to the nectar from local plants. Honey even can help soothe a sore throat!

Skin Care Benefits

Honey is added to many products, anything from moisturizer to soap because it naturally provides and retains moisture. Add it to your own personal care products!
