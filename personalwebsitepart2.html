Bio

With inherent creative and strategic communication skills, Brooke naturally understands clients’ unspoken needs, and creates perfectly tailored messaging. Intrigued by how speech, media, the web, and even nonverbal language strengthen personal connections, Brooke believes life is in the details and developing and nurturing relationships is essential for fulfillment and growth. She also has an instinct for web and print design that is always creative, stylish, and cutting edge.

An American Studies major with an emphasis in public relations and marketing at Willamette University, Brooke excels at event planning and community outreach. When not keeping up on the latest trends in marketing, Brooke can be found soaking up the California sun in her hometown of Stanford. She enjoys hiking and biking, and is an avid baker constantly on the lookout for new and delicious recipes to test.
