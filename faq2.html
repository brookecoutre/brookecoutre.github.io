FAQs

Please contact us if your questions have still not been answered

What is raw honey?

Raw honey comes in its original form as the bess created it: pure, unheated, unprocessed.

Do you ship the honey?

Yes, we can ship locally in California as we encourage people to buy local honey.

Can BnE Honey be bought from store?

No, we are a small, family run business who choose to sell to friends, family, and beyond.

Can I come visit BnE Honey to see the bees?

Yes, but please call ahead of time to set up an appointment.

Does honey spoil?

No, it will not spoil if left in it's natural state and in a sealed container.

Why has my honey turned into solid form?

Your honey is going through a crystalization process. To uncrystalize, take the lid of the jar and put it in pot of hot water. Make sure the honey is in a glass jar and not a blastic honey bear.
