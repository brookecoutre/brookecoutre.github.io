Learn more about us...

We are a family run business from Stanford, California. Beekeeping is a hobby that our family has enjoyed for many years. The beekeeping all started when a friend of ours, who kept beehives on their roof, was moving. His family gifted us our first hive and we have been hooked ever since. We really have enjoyed learning about bees and finding best practices for beekeeping.

We typically have about six beehives in our backyard with thousands of bees who enjoy the local lavendar.

Our honey is featured seasonally on the cheese plate at a local in Menlo Park Resturant.

The significance of our name stemmed from our children's initial's, Brooke and Evan to create BnE.
