BnE Honey

Local Honey from California

Honey is a naturally sweet substance created by honey bees from the nectar of flowers and plants. BnE Honey is a natural product harvested from nature and can have many benefits to people .

Try Our Honey

Natural Sweetner

Honey is a natural sweetner which means less sugar can be added to achieve the same sweetness as sugar. Honey goes great in your cup of tea or even in your recipes.

Fights Allergies

Buying and consuming local honey in the area you reside helps fight allergies by letting your immune system adjust to the nectar from local plants. Honey even can help soothe a sore throat!

Skin Care Benefits

Honey is added to many products, anything from moisturizer to soap because it naturally provides and retains moisture. Add it to your own personal care products!
